PAST COMPETITIONS

Arvind Sridhar 20' and Jack Stone 20' created Altus Water

Altus Water is an environmentally driven company dedicated to solving the problem of excess water usage in homes around the world. Our team identified showers as one of the main culprits of water wastage in the home, and created our flagship product, Rivulet, as a means of combatting inefficient and excess showering that consumes, on average, 1.2 trillion combined gallons of water in the United States each year (source: EPA). Rivulet is a data-driven, Bluetooth-enabled smart showerhead that uses colored LEDs to communicate how long a shower is taking to the user. The showerhead is also able to use ultrasonic proximity sensor technology to reduce water flow while a user is lathering or waiting for their shower to warm up. A mobile app interface complements the showerhead, with the app’s features consisting of a display of water usage statistics, allowing the user to set shower times by changing how quickly the LEDs change color, and displaying the user’s financial savings and environmental impact while using the product.
